How to play?


This is a really easy game for you to challenge yourself and test your knowledge with flags

How many flags can you guess in a short time?

In the Easy mode you have 1 minute to know as many flags as possible!

In the Hard mode you have 40 seconds to know as many flags as possible!

The rules are easy, all you have to do is:


  • Enter your nickname
  • Choose the right answer from a list of 3.
  • You have 1 extra attempt in the Easy mode.
  • Answer as many as you can.
